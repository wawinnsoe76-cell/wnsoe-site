About Wawin NSOE

Wawin National School of Excellence (NSOE) is the premier learning community in Papua New Guinea, committed to nurturing academic excellence, character, and service. Our students are prepared to become leaders in PNG and beyond through holistic education, leadership programs, and co-curricular activities.

Established to provide quality education, Wawin NSOE offers a supportive environment where students thrive academically, socially, and morally.

Admissions

Admission to Wawin NSOE is based on merit, aptitude, and potential. Our process ensures that we select students who will thrive in our rigorous academic and character development programs.

  1. Step 1: Submit Application

    Complete the online or paper application form.

  2. Step 2: Entrance Assessment

    Applicants are assessed through academic and aptitude tests.

  3. Step 3: Interview & Acceptance

    Successful candidates are invited for an interview before acceptance.
